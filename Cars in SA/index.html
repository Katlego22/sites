Stance In South Africa

South Africa is home to
many beautiful cars of all shapes
and sizes.A very popular car modification culture in
South Africa is called the
"Stance" culture.Cars come from all
over the world and the people
of South Africa have their
own beautiful ways of modifying cars.

Stance Events

Stance events have become a major thing within the stance culture. Many people come from all over the land come together to showcase their beautiful rides. Stance events are not only about bringing your cars to showcase it, it's also about coming together to have fun with the people whom you share the same passion with. Food, beverages, jumping castles, water slides and more things are available at stance events so that everyone can enjoy. Whether young or old, everyone is gurenteed to have a good time at stance events
